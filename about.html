About

Hello, I'm working on this personal website a place to share my ideas and throughts about the topics I care about

My name is a Matt Coleman, a native of southern California and current resident of San Francisco. This iteration of my website is a third or fourth renditon, as I'm trying to create a site that 1) fits my picky needs of it looking and acting how I want and 2) allows me to easily post my writing so my friends and family members to routinely visit. For those of you who visit The Ringer, The Athletic, and Barstool Sports, you'll see their influences throughout the site.Also, the inspiration for the sidenotes stems from my fascination of Grantland—which regularly used sidenotes throughout all of its posts. (RIP, Grantland)
