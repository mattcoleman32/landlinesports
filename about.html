About Me

My name is a Matt Coleman, a native of southern California and current resident of San Francisco. This iteration of my website is a third or fourth renditon, as I'm trying to create a site that 1) fits my picky needs of it looking and acting how I want and 2) allows me to easily post my writing so my friends and family members to routinely visit.
